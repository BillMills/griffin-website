Collaboration Structure

The GRIFFIN Collaboration consists of a consortium of institutional members and a team of physicists and engineers from across Canada and abroad. GRIFFIN is administered by its Steering Committee, and codified by its Collaboration Bylaws.

Steering Committee

GRIFFIN recognizes institutional membership of the University of Guelph, TRIUMF, and Simon Fraser University, and is administrated via a Steering Committee consisting of institutional members and an elected user representative.
